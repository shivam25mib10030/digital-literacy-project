Do's 

Do enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.

Do use a professional headshot for LinkedIn.

Do fact-check news before sharing or reposting.

Do adjust privacy settings to "Friends Only" on personal apps.

Do follow industry leaders and companies in your field.


Don'ts

Don't post sensitive info like your ID card or address.

Don't engage in online arguments or "flame wars."

Don't use unprofessional language in public comments.

Don't tag others in photos without their permission.

Don't click on suspicious "clickbait" links or ads.
