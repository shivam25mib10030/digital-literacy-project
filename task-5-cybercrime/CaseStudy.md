Case Study: The "Request Money" UPI Scam


What it is:

A type of social engineering fraud where scammers exploit the "Request" feature on UPI apps.

How it happens:

The attacker lists a fake product/service on a marketplace.

They contact the victim, claiming they want to send a "refund" or "advance payment".

Instead of sending money, the attacker sends a "Pay Request" through an app like GPay or PhonePe.

The victim, thinking they are "accepting" money, enters their UPI PIN.

The transaction is authorized, and money is instantly deducted from the victim's account.


Target:

Frequent online shoppers, students, and people selling items on second-hand platforms.


Consequences:

Instant financial loss, compromised account security, and emotional stress.
