8 actionable tips:

UPI PIN Rule: Remember that a PIN is only required to send money, never to receive it.

Verify Requests: Always double-check the name and VPA (Virtual Payment Address) before approving any request.

Use 2FA: Enable Two-Factor Authentication on all social media and email accounts.Avoid 

Public Wi-Fi: Never conduct banking or sensitive transactions on free, unsecured public Wi-Fi networks.

Check URLs: Look for "https://" and the padlock icon; avoid clicking links in "urgent" SMS messages.

Strong Passwords: Use unique, complex passwords for every account rather than repeating one simple password.

Limit Over-sharing: Don't post your phone number, address, or travel plans publicly on social media.

Official Reporting: If defrauded, immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930
